🔒 Privacy: Your Data Belongs to You

No server upload: All files and text are processed locally in your browser, never sent to any server.

No data storage: Raw data is not persisted after analysis. Interest profiles are saved only in your browser localStorage for cross-page display.

AI analysis optional: If enabled, titles are sent to Anthropic API (requires your own Key or server proxy). Keyword engine analysis runs entirely locally.

Clear anytime: Closing the page, clearing browser data, or clicking "New Analysis" removes your data.
